4.0 Recommended

Psychology Today's therapist directory is the most comprehensive in the United States — tens of thousands of licensed therapists filterable by specialty, insurance, location, approach, and identity. The directory is free to search and use. What Psychology Today does not provide is any platform infrastructure: there's no integrated booking, no video sessions through the site, no messaging, and no billing. You find a therapist, get their contact information, and reach out directly. For people who want maximum control over finding a therapist who fits a very specific profile — particular specialty, insurance, location, or approach — the breadth of the directory is unmatched. For people who want an end-to-end platform experience, it's not that.

Pricing & Plans

Psychology Today's directory is free. Therapist rates vary entirely by individual provider — typically -300 per session depending on specialty, location, and whether they accept insurance. Insurance acceptance is set by each therapist. You negotiate and pay the therapist directly.

per session starting

The directory's value is breadth and control. If you have specific requirements — a therapist who specializes in a rare condition, accepts a specific insurance, and is within five miles — nowhere else gives you that combination at scale.

Who Psychology Today Is Best For

Finding a therapist by specialty, location, insurance, or approach with maximum control
People with specific requirements who need to filter across a large pool of providers
Those who prefer to vet therapists themselves rather than use an algorithm

Who Should Look Elsewhere

If you want integrated booking, billing, and sessions in one app — use Headway or Grow Therapy
If you want a subscription model with ongoing features between sessions
If you want the platform to handle insurance billing — Psychology Today doesn't

Session Formats

Session formats are determined by individual therapists and include video, phone, and in-person depending on the provider. Psychology Today does not provide a platform for sessions — you use whatever method your chosen therapist uses for their practice.
