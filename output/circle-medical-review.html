3.9 Recommended

Circle Medical is a primary care telehealth practice — not a dedicated mental health platform. Mental health services are part of what Circle Medical providers offer alongside urgent care, chronic disease management, and general medicine. The appeal is consolidation: if you want to see one provider who can manage your anxiety, your blood pressure, and your annual physical without hopping between platforms, Circle Medical makes that possible. Most major insurance plans are accepted, and the per-visit cost with insurance is comparable to a standard office copay. The limitation is depth — Circle Medical's mental health providers are generalists, not specialists, and ongoing weekly therapy is not the model.

Insurance-based (-50 copay) Insurance: Most major plans Video

Pricing & Plans

Circle Medical operates on a standard insurance billing model. With most major plans, you'll pay your normal telehealth copay — typically -50 per visit. Self-pay rates are available but priced closer to traditional medical visits. The platform is best valued as a covered medical service, not a self-pay therapy subscription.

copay with insurance

The case for Circle Medical is consolidation — fewer platforms, fewer logins, and mental health treated as a component of whole-person care rather than a separate system.

Who Circle Medical Is Best For

People who want mental health integrated with general primary care
Insured patients who want a single telehealth provider for multiple needs
Those with mild mental health concerns who don't need a dedicated therapist

Who Should Look Elsewhere

Anyone wanting deep ongoing therapy work with a dedicated specialist
People with complex psychiatric needs or specialized conditions
Those who want messaging, coaching, or async between-session support

Session Formats

All appointments are video-based, conducted through the Circle Medical app or browser. There is no messaging tier, no async support, and no phone option. Each appointment is a standalone clinical encounter, not an ongoing care program.
