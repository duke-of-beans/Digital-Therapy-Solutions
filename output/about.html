The origin

Why this site exists

David Kirsch built Digital Therapy Solutions after spending weeks trying to figure out which online therapy platform was right for a family member. Every site he found either recommended everything ("all platforms are great!") or led with affiliate links without disclosing them. The pricing was vague. The "reviews" were barely distinguishable from the platforms' own marketing copy.

"Every review site I found was either clueless or corrupted. The right answer was to build the one that wasn't."

The mission became simple: build the site that actually does the work. Test the platforms. Verify the pricing. Check the insurance coverage. Then write what we found — clearly, with tradeoffs named, with "who should look elsewhere" sections that other sites are too scared to include.

How we work

Our methodology

We sign up for every platform we review. We go through the intake process, get matched, and verify pricing at the payment screen — not by trusting the marketing copy. When a platform says "$65/week," we go to checkout.

01
Hands-on testing
We sign up, go through intake, verify pricing at checkout, and test the matching algorithm.
02
Insurance verification
We check coverage via the platform's tool and by calling carriers directly. We document where they differ.
03
Clinical review
Every review is sent to a licensed mental health professional to verify therapeutic accuracy.
04
Honest scoring
Our rubric covers pricing transparency, therapist quality signals, insurance access, and cancellation terms.

Our commitment to you

We earn affiliate commissions when you sign up through our links. We disclose this clearly on every page. These commissions pay for our research and keep the site free. They do not influence our rankings — we've recommended platforms we don't have affiliate relationships with, and we've written critical reviews of platforms we do.

No platform has ever paid us for a favorable review. No platform has ever been told in advance what their score would be. If that ever changes, we'll close the site.
