Why Condition-Specific Guidance Matters

Choosing a therapy platform without knowing whether it specializes in your condition is like choosing a doctor without knowing their specialty. A platform built for general talk therapy may not have enough therapists trained in OCD treatment, trauma-informed EMDR, or eating disorder recovery. Our condition guides go beyond "does this platform offer therapy?" — they tell you which platforms have the right practitioners, the right modalities, and the right track record for what you're dealing with.
