Talkspace

Best if insurance is covering your burnout recovery

4.3 Recommended

Talkspace's insurance coverage — Aetna, BCBS, Cigna, and UHC — makes regular burnout therapy sustainable when you're already resource-depleted. Their messaging format suits high-workload individuals who can't reliably block an hour each week. Psychiatry is available if burnout has triggered depression or anxiety requiring medication.

Online-Therapy.com

Best for structured CBT tools to rebuild capacity

4.8 Recommended

Online-Therapy.com's structured CBT program addresses the cognitive patterns — perfectionism, difficulty saying no, catastrophizing — that often underlie burnout. The structured worksheets and journaling tools create space for reflection that people in burnout rarely give themselves. At $45–80/week, it's accessible for out-of-pocket payers.
