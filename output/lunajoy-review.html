4.1 Recommended

LunaJoy is one of the few telehealth platforms built specifically around the mental health needs of women across different life stages. The platform combines therapists and psychiatric providers who specialize in perinatal mental health, postpartum depression, PMDD, perimenopause-related mood changes, and hormonal transitions. Several insurance plans are accepted, which makes it accessible for postpartum care where insurance coverage is often more reliably available. For women navigating mental health challenges tied to reproductive health events — pregnancy, the postpartum period, or perimenopause — LunaJoy offers a level of clinical specialization that general platforms can't match.

Insurance-based Insurance: Several plans Video

Pricing & Plans

LunaJoy operates on an insurance-based model for most services. Several plans are accepted, including some Medicaid plans that cover perinatal mental health. Out-of-pocket costs depend on your coverage. Verify your specific plan before starting, as state availability and in-network status vary.

Postpartum depression affects one in five new mothers. Having access to a platform where every provider understands that context — rather than having to explain it — is clinically significant.

Who LunaJoy Is Best For

Women seeking care attuned to hormonal mental health and postpartum depression
People navigating perinatal anxiety, PMDD, or perimenopause-related mood changes
Women who want both therapy and psychiatry on a specialized platform

Who Should Look Elsewhere

General therapy needs not related to women's hormonal health
Men or people who don't identify as women
Those needing nationwide coverage — LunaJoy's availability varies by state

Session Formats

All sessions are video-based. LunaJoy's clinical model involves regular video appointments with your therapist and separate check-ins with your psychiatric provider if medication is part of your care plan. The platform is video-first with no async messaging option.
