How We Review

Every platform on this page was evaluated through hands-on testing — we sign up, go through the matching process, assess the therapist network, and verify pricing ourselves. Our scores are based on therapist quality and availability, session format options, pricing transparency, ease of use, and how well the platform handles insurance or financial aid. No platform pays to be listed, and no platform can pay to improve its score. When we accept affiliate commissions, we disclose them — and they never influence rankings.
