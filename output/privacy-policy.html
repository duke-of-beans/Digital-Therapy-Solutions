Privacy Policy

Plain English. No tracking pixels. No data selling. No legalese.

Last Updated: March 2026 Plain Language No Tracking CCPA Compliant
Updated March 19, 2026

The short version

We don't collect personal information. We don't sell data. We don't run advertising tracking pixels. We use a privacy-first analytics tool (Plausible or Fathom — we'll update this when we deploy analytics) that gives us aggregate site traffic without tracking individual users. The only data that touches our servers is standard web server logs.

What we collect (very little)

Standard web server logs: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time of visit. These logs are used only for server security and are automatically deleted after 30 days. We do not use these logs to build individual user profiles.

Contact form/email: If you email us, we receive your email address and message. We use this only to respond to your inquiry. We don't add you to any mailing lists, and we don't share your email with third parties.

What we don't collect

We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any advertising tracking technology. We don't run retargeting ads. We don't have a mailing list or newsletter (yet — if we add one, this policy will be updated and it will always be opt-in). We don't use cookies for tracking purposes.

Affiliate links

When you click an affiliate link to an online therapy platform, that platform may use their own tracking technology to credit us with the referral. This tracking is controlled by the platform, not by us. We can't speak to those platforms' privacy practices — please review their individual privacy policies before signing up.

Analytics

We intend to use Plausible Analytics or Fathom Analytics — both are GDPR-compliant, privacy-first tools that don't track individual users, don't use cookies, and don't send data to Google. When we deploy analytics, we'll update this page with specifics.

Your rights

If you've contacted us and want us to delete your email from our records, email us at contact@digitaltherapysolutions.com and we'll comply within 30 days. Since we don't have accounts, mailing lists, or stored profiles, there's nothing else to delete.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We won't add tracking or data collection without clear notice.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at contact@digitaltherapysolutions.com

We're not lawyers. This policy is written in plain English, not legal boilerplate. If you have specific legal questions about data handling, consult a qualified attorney.
