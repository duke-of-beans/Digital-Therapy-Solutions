How Coverage Works

Whether online therapy is covered depends on three things: your specific plan, your state's parity laws, and which platform your insurer has contracted with. The Mental Health Parity and Addiction Equity Act requires most insurance plans to cover mental health at the same level as physical health — but "covered" doesn't always mean "covered on this platform." Our guides break down which platforms each insurer works with, what typical copays look like, and what to ask your insurer before you start.
