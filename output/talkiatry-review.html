Talkiatry is a psychiatric group practice built for the telehealth era, with deep insurance integrations across most major carriers. The platform offers psychiatric evaluation, medication management, and therapy — all in-network with most major plans. What differentiates Talkiatry from general therapy platforms is the psychiatric depth: providers are psychiatrists and psychiatric nurse practitioners, not just therapists who can refer to psychiatry. For insured adults who need psychiatric care — evaluation, medication, or a combination of medication and therapy — Talkiatry is one of the strongest insurance-covered options in the market. The covered copay model makes ongoing psychiatric care financially sustainable in a way that self-pay psychiatry rarely is.

Insurance-based (-100 copay) Insurance: Most major plans Video

Pricing & Plans

Talkiatry accepts most major insurance plans. With coverage, your cost is typically your in-network copay — -100 per appointment depending on your plan and provider type. Self-pay rates are available but not prominently published. The insurance model is the platform's primary value proposition.

copay starting with insurance

Finding an in-network psychiatrist through a traditional directory is one of the more frustrating experiences in healthcare. Talkiatry's model is built specifically to close that gap.

Who Talkiatry Is Best For

Insured adults seeking psychiatric evaluation, medication, and/or ongoing therapy
People with depression, anxiety, ADHD, or mood disorders who need both medication and therapy
Those who've been unable to find an in-network psychiatrist through traditional channels

Who Should Look Elsewhere

If you don't need psychiatry — a therapy-only platform will be more cost-effective
Self-pay patients without insurance — Talkiatry's model is insurance-optimized
Those needing highly specialized subspecialty psychiatry beyond general adult practice

Session Formats

All Talkiatry appointments are conducted via video. There is no in-person option and no async messaging tier. Sessions are structured clinical encounters — psychiatric evaluations, medication management check-ins, and therapy sessions as applicable to your care plan.
