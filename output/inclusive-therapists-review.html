Inclusive Therapists is a therapist directory founded on the premise that identity-affirming care isn't a nice-to-have — it's a clinical necessity for many clients. The directory filters for LGBTQ+-affirming, BIPOC-affirming, disability-affirming, and neurodivergent-affirming therapists, with sliding scale options prioritized. Unlike general directories, Inclusive Therapists vets listed providers for actual cultural competency, not just self-reported affirmation. You browse profiles, filter by the identities and specialties that matter to you, and contact therapists directly. Insurance acceptance varies by individual provider.

There is a meaningful difference between a therapist who is not actively harmful to LGBTQ+ clients and a therapist who has genuinely developed cultural competency in affirming care. Inclusive Therapists is built around that distinction.

Who Inclusive Therapists Is Best For

LGBTQ+, BIPOC, disabled, or neurodivergent people seeking affirming therapists
Anyone who has had experiences of their identity being minimized or pathologized in therapy
People who want sliding scale access and identity-affirming care together

Who Should Look Elsewhere

If you want a subscription model with messaging and platform features
If you need insurance billing handled centrally — contact each therapist directly for coverage
If you need psychiatry or medication management alongside therapy

Session Formats

Session formats are set by individual therapists and include video and in-person depending on location. There is no centralized platform for booking or sessions — you connect with therapists directly through their contact information on the directory.
