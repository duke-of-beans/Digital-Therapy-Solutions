4.1 Recommended

Teen Counseling is BetterHelp's platform for teenagers ages 13 to 19. The matching algorithm filters for therapists who specialize in adolescent mental health — school stress, social anxiety, identity development, family conflict, and the specific challenges of teenage experience. Parents can be connected to the therapy room with the teen's consent, creating an option for parental visibility without mandating it. The platform infrastructure is identical to BetterHelp — video, phone, chat, messaging — with the same financial aid options. Insurance is not accepted. For parents seeking online therapy for their teenager and willing to pay out of pocket, Teen Counseling is a well-built option with appropriate adolescent safeguards.

Pricing & Plans

Teen Counseling mirrors BetterHelp's pricing: -100/week on a 4-week billing cycle. Insurance is not accepted. Financial aid is available for qualifying families through the same application as BetterHelp. Parents are the account holders and manage billing; the teen is the client.

per week starting

Giving teenagers a degree of autonomy over their therapy experience — including whether their parents can see the conversation — increases the likelihood they'll actually engage with treatment.

Who Teen Counseling Is Best For

Teenagers ages 13-19 needing therapy for anxiety, stress, or identity-related challenges
Parents seeking mental health support for their teen that the teen can access independently
Families comfortable with out-of-pocket costs who want fast matching

Who Should Look Elsewhere

If insurance coverage is needed — Teen Counseling doesn't accept insurance
For children under 13 — use Brightline, Bend Health, or Manatee Health
If you want an in-person or hybrid option alongside online sessions

Session Formats

Teen Counseling offers video sessions, phone sessions, and async messaging — the full BetterHelp format suite. The teen chooses how to communicate with their therapist. Parents can be added to the therapy room with the teen's explicit consent, allowing for periodic family sessions or updates.
