Talkspace

Best for UHC coverage — in-network with most UnitedHealthcare and Optum plans

4.6 Top Rated

Talkspace has a direct partnership with Optum — UHC's behavioral health arm — making them one of the most seamlessly covered platforms for UnitedHealthcare members. Most UHC members pay $10–25 per session, a significant drop from the $69–109/week self-pay rate. They offer both therapy and psychiatry, and the Optum integration means your coverage verification is typically instant. The tradeoff: the messaging-based format may feel impersonal compared to video-first platforms, and some older UHC plans may not include Talkspace in-network.
