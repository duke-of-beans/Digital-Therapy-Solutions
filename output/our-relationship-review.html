4.1 Recommended

Our Relationship is not a therapy platform — it's a structured self-guided program for couples, developed by researchers at the University of Chicago. The program is based on decades of relationship science and walks couples through a series of interactive modules, exercises, and guided reflections designed to improve communication, reduce conflict, and increase relationship satisfaction. At one-time, it's the most affordable intervention for couples who want structured support. The evidence base is real — multiple randomized controlled trials show meaningful improvement in relationship satisfaction for couples who complete the program. The important caveat: this is not a substitute for live couples therapy if there are serious issues that require clinical intervention.

one-time No insurance accepted Self-guided program

Pricing & Plans

Our Relationship costs as a one-time fee — no subscriptions, no per-session billing. The program is fully self-paced and can be completed at whatever speed works for the couple. The one-time cost model makes it accessible as a starting point for couples who are hesitant to commit to ongoing therapy costs.

one-time program fee

The research behind Our Relationship is solid — this isn't self-help content dressed up as a program. For couples who want structure without the cost of ongoing sessions, the evidence base makes it worth considering.

Who Our Relationship Is Best For

Couples wanting a structured, affordable program to improve communication
Partners who want to work on their relationship before problems become serious
Those who prefer a self-paced program over scheduled live therapy sessions

Who Should Look Elsewhere

If you need live couples therapy — this is a program, not a therapist match
Couples in acute crisis who need clinical intervention, not a self-guided course
Anyone expecting ongoing therapist contact or real-time support

Session Formats

Our Relationship is entirely self-guided — there are no live sessions, no therapist contact, and no real-time interactions. The program is delivered through a web-based interface with video content, written exercises, and interactive modules. Couples complete it together on their own schedule.
