Platforms We Recommend for Autism & Neurodivergence

Each platform below was personally tested and clinically reviewed by Dr. Sarah Chen, LCSW — a licensed therapist with experience in neurodiversity-affirming care.

Grow Therapy

Best for finding neurodiversity-affirming therapists — searchable by specialty

4.4 Top Rated

Grow Therapy's profile-browse model is the right fit here — you can search specifically for therapists with autism or neurodivergence experience, read their bios, and verify their approach before booking. For autistic adults, therapist fit matters more than platform features. Insurance-based pricing brings the cost down significantly for most plans.

BetterHelp

Best for access and flexibility — async messaging suits many autistic clients

4.3 Recommended

BetterHelp's messaging format works well for autistic clients who prefer written communication, need more processing time, or find video sessions draining. The platform lets you specify therapist preferences including neurodivergence experience. The large network means you can switch if the first match isn't right. $65–100/week; financial aid available.

$65–100/week Financial aid available Video Phone Messaging (async)
✦ Async messaging option — communicate on your own schedule Find a Therapist on BetterHelp

Inclusive Therapists

Best directory for marginalized identities — neurodivergent-affirming focus

4.2 Recommended

Inclusive Therapists is a directory specifically built for people seeking therapists who affirm their identity — including neurodivergent and autistic clients. You browse profiles, filter by neurodiversity affirmation, and connect directly. Pricing varies by therapist; many offer sliding scale rates.
