# Icons Ban — Digital Therapy Solutions

**Decision:** Emoji are permanently banned from all pages on this site.

**Reason:** Emoji clash with the "authoritative warmth" design language and undermine credibility. They look amateur in the context of a professionally-positioned health information site.

**Standard:** All icons must be cohesive inline SVG, stroke-based, humanist style.
- Single-color stroke SVGs only (not filled/solid)
- Stroke color: `var(--accent-primary)` or `var(--accent-warm)`
- Size: 32×32 viewBox, rendered at 28–32px
- Style: organic, slightly imperfect — humanist, not tech/SaaS

**Applied first in:** PS-DESIGN-QA-01 (March 2026)
**Enforced across:** index.html condition tiles, conditions.html hub icons, all future pages
